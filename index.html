About Us

We are a passionate community of gamers dedicated to providing a fun, inclusive, and supportive environment. Whether you're new to gaming or a seasoned player, our community is here to welcome you!

Our mission is to foster connections among gamers from all walks of life, ensuring that everyone feels valued and included. We believe in the power of gaming to bring people together, allowing for shared experiences and lifelong friendships.

We organize a variety of events, ranging from casual game nights to competitive tournaments. Our team works tirelessly to ensure that each event is tailored to meet the interests of our members, providing a space where everyone can thrive.

  • Weekly game nights featuring different genres to keep the excitement alive.
  • Monthly tournaments featuring fun challenges and friendly rivalry, where you can showcase your skills and make lasting memories with fellow gamers.
  • A friendly and helpful community to enhance your gaming experience.
  • Professional administration ensuring a safe and fun environment for all.

Frequently Asked Questions

What games do you play?

We play a variety of games, including FPS, RPG, strategy, and indie titles.

How can I participate in events?

You can sign up for events in the designated channels on our Discord server.

Is there an age limit?

While we encourage a mature environment, we welcome gamers of all ages!

How do I join the community?

Joining is easy! Just click the "Join Our Discord" button above.

I cannot write on the server and join voice channels - what should I do?

You need to verify yourself through our PandOS bot. Send a private message with $VerifyMe, and then use $start <code> that you will receive.

Will I get banned immediately for violating the rules?

We are flexible in our approach. We have a warning system in place. However, we do not tolerate hatred, pedophilia, or other harmful behavior. Jokes to lighten the mood are fine as long as they don't harm anyone.

Can I invite my friends or do I need permission?

Feel free to invite your friends as long as they are responsible and don’t cause chaos in the community.

Can I share content or media in the server?

Yes, you can share content in the appropriate channels, but make sure it follows the server guidelines and doesn’t violate any rules.

How can I report a problem with the server?

Just write a private message to the PandOS Bot and we will read it and fix what you need.

How do I know if my bot is working properly?

Test your bot by using commands like $ping or checking the bot's status in the designated channels.
